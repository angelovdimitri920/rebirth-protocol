# Canon Ledger and Development Backlog

*Authority register, contradiction tracker, and creative-development queue. Consolidated baseline: 2026-07-23.*

## 1. Purpose

This ledger prevents proposals, implementation history, and intentional mysteries from silently becoming canon.

Use it to:

- Record locked facts.
- Track drafts.
- Preserve mysteries.
- Resolve contradictions.
- Identify superseded terms and documents.
- Sequence future CANON merges.

This is not the gameplay implementation ladder. Current code tasks remain in [`TASK_LADDER.md`](TASK_LADDER.md).

## 2. Authority order

When sources conflict, use:

1. Explicit user-approved decision.
2. The numbered document that owns the subject.
3. A canonical specialist document within its stated scope.
4. Current implementation for what the game actually does.
5. Mixed legacy documents.
6. Research, migration, or superseded references.

Never use implementation alone to settle a fictional mystery or creative decision.

## 3. Locked canon ledger

### Theme and structure

- Working title: **Rebirth Protocol**.
- Genre structure: story-driven 3D arena mecha action with a JRPG-style campaign shell and roguelite Passage events.
- Structural phrase: **A JRPG-shaped game with a Western soul.**
- Central question: **Can humanity safely possess power that only works through obedience?**
- Campaign spine: **Circuit → Broken Choir → First Armiger → Cradle**.
- Final game is not a menu-only roguelite.

### History

- Meridian Ascendancy as globally spanning First Empire.
- War of the Broken Seals.
- Final Edict at year zero.
- Twilight Years.
- Great Severance.
- Long Hush.
- Warring States.
- Twin Restorations.
- War of the Two Mandates.
- Ashen Century.
- Concord of Ash.
- Roughly 800-year Present Age.
- Recent War of the Open Forges.

### Technology

- Firstwork, Restoration work, relic-pattern, Revival-pattern, and scrapwright origins.
- Severed and Edictbound as command conditions.
- Edictbound as active Final Edict infection in technology of any era.
- Pilot cybernetics, synchronization, and progressive Edictbound decline.
- Rebirth flare as a local fragment of a larger continuity protocol.
- Riderless as an imperial executor waiting in the Cradle.
- No broad public robot civilization.

### Institutions

- Universal Communion of the Severed Crown.
- Court of Ten Thousand Banners.
- Covenant of the Second Sun and Mortal Measure.
- Rite of the Whole Crown and Veiled Restoration.
- Broken Choir.
- Ash Levelers and Cinderbound.
- Brass Testament as disputed apocryphon, not established machine church.
- Major military Orders, Wrightsguild, and Litany Sisters.

### Gameplay

- Modular body, right arm, left arm, pod, and legs.
- Right arm gun or melee.
- Left arm bomb or shield.
- Casting, neutral, knockdown, rebirth rhythm.
- Full invulnerability while downed.
- Rebirth invulnerability on standing.
- Overload wipes the fallen pilot's gun rounds.
- Chassis and parts must change playstyle.
- Grand Passage and Passage of Arms terminology.
- Laurels, Edictbound laurel penalty, and C-to-S armiger ranks.
- Equal-duel targets of 60-to-120 seconds and 2-to-5 knockdowns.

## 10. Recommended creative-development order

1. Approve **Theme and Premise**.
2. Audit and approve **Historical Foundation**.
3. Audit and approve **Technology and Setting Rules**.
4. Build **Present-Day World and Atlas**, beginning with Lasthold.
5. Place **Powers, Faiths, and Factions** into that geography.
6. Create the protagonist and core **Characters and Relationships**.
7. Build the **Campaign and Narrative**.
8. Resolve **Gameplay, Tournament, and Progression** around that campaign.
9. Run a final canon and consistency audit.

This dependency chain remains:

> **Theme → History and technology → Present world → Powers → Characters → Campaign → Gameplay progression → Audit**

## 13. Final audit checklist

- Every fact has one authoritative home.
- Drafts are labeled.
- Mysteries remain unresolved.
- No source-specific names survive as accidental canon.
- Timeline dates agree.
- Factions' motives remain distinct.
- Character origins agree with the atlas.
- Campaign beats agree with relationships.
- Gameplay rewards agree with story ownership and law.
- Current engine and repository instructions no longer point to obsolete authority.
- All Markdown links resolve.
- No useful legacy detail is deleted before migration is verified.
